Xianjie Dai

I am a Research Assistant at KAUST, where I work with Prof. Mohamed Elhoseiny at Vision-CAIR and collaborate closely with Jian Ding on generative models and embodied AI. My current research focuses on end-to-end vision-language and vision-language-action models.

I received my M.S. degree in Computer Science from EPFL. Before that, I obtained my bachelor's degree from The Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

Research

My current work focuses on in-context learning and generalization in embodied AI. More broadly, I am interested in multimodal learning, vision-language models, representation learning, and reliable decision-making systems.
